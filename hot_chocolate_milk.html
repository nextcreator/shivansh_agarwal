  • HOT CHOCOLATE MILK

Ingrediants

  • Milk(of yor choice).
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar.
  • White/dark chocolate chips.
  • Vanilla extract.
  • Garnish of your choice.

Procedure

  1. Place the milk of your choice in a saucepan over medium-low heat.
  2. Add in cocoa powder and sugar, and heat until warm.
  3. Once the milk is warm, add chocolate chips, whisking until they melt into the milk.
  4. Add a splash of vanilla extract.
  5. Now take it of the heat. Serve immediately and top with your favorite garnishes: marshmallows, whipped cream, chopped chocolate, crushed candy canes and more.
  6. Enjoy!!
